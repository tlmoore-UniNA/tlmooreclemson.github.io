Thomas L. Moore, Ph.D.

I am a research fellow at the Istituto Italiano di Tecnologia (IIT) in Genoa, Italy. My background is in Bioengineering — I obtained my B.Sc. in Bioengineering (Biomaterials) and Ph.D. in Bioengineering (Nanomedicine) from Clemson University (Clemson, SC, USA). In 2014, I moved to Fribourg, Switzerland and joined the BioNanomaterials group at the Adolphe Merkle Institute.
